Hubble Future Plans

Is the Hubble Still In Use?

Yes, the Hubble is still in use, and will continue to operate until failure.

Future of Hubble?

  • In the past, Hubble had regular servicing missions using the Space Shuttle fleet. However, the Space Shuttle was retired, so the Hubble will no longer receive servicing missions.
  • While Hubble is still operational, it will continue to carry out its mission and complement the James Webb Space Telescope by conducting observations in the visible and ultraviolet spectrums.
  • Once Hubble's mission ends, which will likely to be due to its gyros, NASA will prepare Hubble for a controlled reentry into the Earth's atmosphere.
